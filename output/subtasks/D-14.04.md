# D-14.04 | Finishes soups

**Major Work Activity:** D — Stocks, Broths and Soups
**Task:** D-14 | Prepares soups

## Skills

### D-14.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** adjust soup characteristics

**Evidence of Attainment:** soup characteristics are adjusted to achieve desired results

### D-14.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** cool soup

**Evidence of Attainment:** soup is cooled according to jurisdictional regulations and desired results

### D-14.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** prepare finishing ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** finishing ingredients are prepared using methods according to recipe specifications and desired results

### D-14.04.04P
**Performance Criteria:** garnish soup

**Evidence of Attainment:** soup is garnished according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- soup characteristics include: taste, thickness, texture, appearance, colour, temperature
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### D-14.04.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of soups and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with soups
- identify types of soups, and describe their characteristics and serving temperatures
- identify factors to consider when assembling soups
- identify dietary requirements specific to soups

### D-14.04.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to complete soups

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to cool soups
- describe methods used to prepare finishing ingredients
- describe presentation and finishing techniques

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- types of soups include: clear, consommés, cream, puréed, specialty/international, cold
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- methods include: blanching, sautéing, roasting, steaming, boiling
