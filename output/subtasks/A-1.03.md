# A-1.03 | Maintains personal hygiene

**Major Work Activity:** A — Hygiene, sanitation and safety
**Task:** A-1 | Performs safety and hygiene-related functions

## Skills

### A-1.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** follow handwashing procedures

**Evidence of Attainment:** handwashing procedures are followed according to jurisdictional regulations

### A-1.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain good personal hygienic conditions

**Evidence of Attainment:** good personal hygienic conditions are maintained according to company policy and jurisdictional regulations

### A-1.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** wear clean work attire

**Evidence of Attainment:** clean work attire is worn

### A-1.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** wear hair and beard nets and hats

**Evidence of Attainment:** hair and beard nets and hats are worn to reduce contamination risks according to jurisdictional regulations

### A-1.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** ensure nails are short

**Evidence of Attainment:** nails are kept short according to company policy and jurisdictional regulations

### A-1.03.06P
**Performance Criteria:** remove jewelry and accessories

**Evidence of Attainment:** jewelry and accessories are removed to reduce contamination risks according to company policy and jurisdictional regulations

### A-1.03.07P
**Performance Criteria:** cover all exposed cuts and wounds

**Evidence of Attainment:** all exposed cuts and wounds are covered according to jurisdictional regulations

## Range of Variables

- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations
- work attire includes: aprons, pants, shirts, jackets, hats, approved footwear

## Knowledge

### A-1.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of personal hygiene principles and practices

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe hygienic practices
- define terminology associated with personal hygiene and hygienic practices

### A-1.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures to maintain personal hygiene

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to ensure personal hygiene and clean apparel
- describe procedures used for hand washing

### A-1.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of jurisdictional regulations pertaining to health and safety

**Learning Objectives:**
- interpret food health regulations and guidelines associated with the practice of personal hygiene
- interpret jurisdictional regulations and company guidelines associated with wearing personal items

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- hygienic practices include: frequent handwashing, wearing clean uniforms, wearing hair and beard nets, using single-use towels, using deodorant, using hair restraints, oral health, frequent bathing
- personal items include: fragrances, jewelry, nail polish, accessories
