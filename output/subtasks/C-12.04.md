# C-12.04 | Cooks fruit

**Major Work Activity:** C — Produce
**Task:** C-12 | Prepares fruit

## Skills

### C-12.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select cooking method

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking method is selected according to fruit classification, desired result and recipe specifications

### C-12.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** apply cooking method

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking method is applied according to desired result

### C-12.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to desired results and recipe specifications

### C-12.04.04P
**Performance Criteria:** preserve nutritional value

**Evidence of Attainment:** nutritional value of fruit is preserved

### C-12.04.05P
**Performance Criteria:** determine doneness

**Evidence of Attainment:** doneness of cooked fruit is determined according to sensory assessment, desired results and recipe specifications

### C-12.04.06P
**Performance Criteria:** apply cooking techniques to market forms of fruits

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking techniques are applied to market forms of fruits according to manufacturers’ specifications

## Range of Variables

- cooking methods include: sautéing, grilling, poaching, frying, baking
- fruit classifications include: tree, citrus, stone, vine, tropical, berry
- tools and equipment include: steamer, frying pan, deep fryer, oven, grill, colander
- market forms include: peeled, dehydrated, whole, cut, bulk frozen, IQF, canned

## Knowledge

### C-12.04.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of cooking fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify cooking methods used to cook fruits
- identify cooking requirements when cooking fruits
- identify degree of doneness for fruits

### C-12.04.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to cook fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe techniques or ingredients used to maintain pigmentation and textures of fruits
- describe procedures used to prepare fruits
- describe techniques used to maintain nutritional integrity of fruit varieties

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- cooking methods include: sautéing, grilling, poaching, frying, baking
- cooking requirements include: temperature, timing, batch size
