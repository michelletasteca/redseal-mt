# B-8.03 | Prepares food to meet dietary requirements

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-8 | Adapts cooking practices to meet dietary requirements

## Skills

### B-8.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** modify recipes

**Evidence of Attainment:** recipes are modified by substituting ingredients to meet dietary requirements

### B-8.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** modify cooking procedures

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking procedures are modified with substitute ingredients while avoiding cross contamination according to dietary requirements

### B-8.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** prepare alternate dish

**Evidence of Attainment:** alternate dish is prepared according to dietary requirements and labelled

### B-8.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** identify dish and advise service team

**Evidence of Attainment:** dish is identified and service team is advised of dietary requirements

## Range of Variables

- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences

## Knowledge

### B-8.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of preparing food to meet dietary requirements

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify food ingredients, allergens and substitutes
- identify factors to consider when selecting alternate ingredients
- identify factors to consider when proposing alternate dish to accommodate dietary requirements

### B-8.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to prepare food to meet dietary requirements

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to prepare food to meet dietary requirements
- describe procedures used to adjust recipe yields and perform conversions

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
