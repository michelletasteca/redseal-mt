# K-29.01 | Selects sandwich ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** K — Salads and Sandwiches
**Task:** K-29 | Prepares sandwiches

## Skills

### K-29.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify ingredients for filling and toppings

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients for filling and toppings are identified according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-29.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** choose bread

**Evidence of Attainment:** bread is chosen according to sandwich style

### K-29.01.03P
**Performance Criteria:** identify indicators of freshness and quality

**Evidence of Attainment:** indicators of freshness and quality are identified according to sensory assessment

## Range of Variables

- bread includes: buns, sliced bread, baguettes, tortillas, pitas, flatbread
- indicators of freshness and quality include: hydration, colour, texture, best before date

## Knowledge

### K-29.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of sandwiches and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with sandwiches
- identify types of sandwiches and describe their characteristics
- identify factors to consider when selecting sandwich ingredients
- identify dietary requirements specific to sandwich ingredients

### K-29.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to select ingredients for sandwiches

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to select ingredients for sandwiches

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- types of sandwiches include: hamburgers, hot dogs, paninis, shawarma, wraps, picnic, finger, pizza, deli
- factors include: types of fillings and toppings, types of bread or alternatives (lettuce wrap), cooking method, assembly sequence, service application
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
