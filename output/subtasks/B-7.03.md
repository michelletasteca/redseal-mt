# B-7.03 | Presents finished product

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-7 | Performs trade activities

## Skills

### B-7.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify menu item components

**Evidence of Attainment:** menu item components are identified to prepare for plating and presentation

### B-7.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools used for plating and presentation

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools used for plating and presentation are selected according to factors

### B-7.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** coordinate cooked product supply

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooked product supply is coordinated for transport, plating and presentation

### B-7.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** determine plating and presentation design, components and plating sequence components and sequence is determined

**Evidence of Attainment:** plating and presentation design, according to factors

### B-7.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** assemble components

**Evidence of Attainment:** components are assembled according to recipe specifications

### B-7.03.06P
**Performance Criteria:** ensure accuracy, consistency and quality of final plating and presentation

**Evidence of Attainment:** accuracy, consistency and quality of final plating and presentation is done according to recipe specifications

### B-7.03.07P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain safe food temperature when plating, presenting and transporting

**Evidence of Attainment:** safe food temperature is maintained when plating, presenting and transporting foods using appropriate equipment according to jurisdictional regulations

### B-7.03.08P
**Performance Criteria:** label packaged foods

**Evidence of Attainment:** packaged foods are labelled according to company policy and jurisdictional regulations

## Range of Variables

- factors include: standardized recipe, style of service, type of food, resources at hand, temperature of food, cost
- transporting foods includes: off-site catering, take-out/delivery service, home meal replacements (HMR)
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### B-7.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of plating and presenting finished products

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify types of service wares
- describe importance of colour, focal point and flow
- describe guidelines for plating and presentation
- explain time required for plating and presentation
- explain importance of required service ware temperature and cleanliness for plating and presentation
- describe required food temperature and holding times
- identify types of presentation styles
- describe time considerations of foods when plating and presenting products

### B-7.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to plate and present finished products

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to plate and present finished products
- describe method used to calculate quantity of service wares required

### B-7.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of packaging finished products

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify types of packaging
- describe required food temperature and holding times

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- service wares include: plates, platters, take-out containers, hotel pans, bowls, ceramic, plastic, steel, mirrors, paper, bamboo, porcelains, slate, hard woods
- types of presentation styles include: center, stacking, flat
