# C-10.02 | Cleans herbs

**Major Work Activity:** C — Produce
**Task:** C-10 | Prepares herbs and spices

## Skills

### C-10.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** clean herbs

**Evidence of Attainment:** herbs are cleaned using methods according to recipe specifications

### C-10.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain quality of herbs

**Evidence of Attainment:** herbs are cleaned gently to maintain quality and freshness

## Range of Variables

- herbs include: rosemary, mint, thyme, basil
- methods include: soaking, rinsing, shaking, drying

## Knowledge

### C-10.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to clean herbs

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe methods used to clean herbs
- describe procedures used to maintain quality of herbs during cleaning
- explain why herb quality and freshness should be maintained during cleaning

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- methods include: soaking, rinsing, shaking, drying
- herbs include: rosemary, mint, thyme, basil
