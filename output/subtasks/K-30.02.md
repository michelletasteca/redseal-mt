# K-30.02 | Processes salad ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** K — Salads and Sandwiches
**Task:** K-30 | Prepares salads

## Skills

### K-30.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use processing tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-30.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** use processing methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing methods are used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-30.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** combine salad ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** salad ingredients are combined according to recipe specifications and salad type

### K-30.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** prepare salad garnishes

**Evidence of Attainment:** salad garnishes are prepared according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-30.02.05P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain safe handling procedures

**Evidence of Attainment:** safe handling procedures are maintained according to jurisdictional regulations

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: knives, spinners, mashers, pots, food processors, cutting boards
- methods include: cleaning, soaking, cooking, drying, cutting, straining, marinating
- ingredients include: vegetables, fruit, proteins, accompaniments, starches, grains, dressings, cheese, pulses, seeds
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### K-30.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of salads and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with salads
- identify types of warm and cold salads, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify factors to consider when processing ingredients for salads
- identify dietary requirements specific to salad ingredients

### K-30.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to process salad ingredients

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe methods used to prepare salad ingredients
- describe procedures used to select salad dressings for desired results
- describe procedures used to assemble salads

### K-30.02.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold and store salads

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold and store salads

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- warm salads include: bowls, compound, potato
- cold salads include: caesar, bean, garden, coleslaw, chicken
- factors include: integrity, texture, viscosity, shelf life, size
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- ingredients include: vegetables, fruit, proteins, accompaniments, starches, grains, dressings, cheese, pulses, seeds
- methods include: cleaning, soaking, cooking, drying, cutting, straining, marinating
