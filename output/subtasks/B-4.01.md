# B-4.01 | Organizes kitchen workplace

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-4 | Organizes work

## Skills

### B-4.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** arrange utensils, equipment, appliances and products

**Evidence of Attainment:** utensils, equipment, appliances and products are arranged for flow of production

### B-4.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** organize and maintain workspace

**Evidence of Attainment:** workspace is organized and maintained according to company policies and production needs

## Range of Variables

- utensils include: knives, wooden spoons, whisks, spatulas, scissors, tongs, tweezers
- equipment and appliances include: meat grinders, slicers, mixers, vacuum packaging machines, thermometers, scales, food processors, blender

## Knowledge

### B-4.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of organizing and identify terminology associated with food maintaining kitchen workspace identify methods used for communication in workplace identify organization and maintenance procedures of kitchen workspace and mise en place

**Learning Objectives:**
- preparation and service procedures

### B-4.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures describe procedures used to plan and used to plan and maintain kitchen workspace

**Learning Objectives:**
- maintain kitchen workspace and mise en place

### B-4.01.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of safe work practices

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with health and safety
- identify potential hazards and describe safe work practices

### B-4.01.04L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of regulatory requirements pertaining to health and safety

**Learning Objectives:**
- interpret jurisdictional regulations pertaining to health and safety

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- methods used for communication include: electronic, verbal, written
- safe work practices include: proper lifting and transferring procedures, safe posture and movements (sitting, standing, rigging, bending)
- potential hazards include: water, debris, exposed electrical wires, uneven floors, chemicals, fire, environmental
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations
