# I-25.03 | Cooks poultry and game birds

**Major Work Activity:** I — Meats, Game Meats, Poultry, Game Birds
**Task:** I-25 | Prepares poultry and game birds

## Skills

### I-25.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and apply cooking methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking methods are selected and applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

### I-25.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** calculate cooking time for poultry and game birds

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking time is calculated according to a combination of factors to ensure even cooking according to recipe specifications and desired results

### I-25.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** determine doneness and tenderness

**Evidence of Attainment:** doneness and tenderness are determined by using various methods according to recipe specifications and jurisdictional regulations

## Range of Variables

- cooking methods include: braising, deep frying, broiling, roasting, combination, sous-vide
- poultry includes: chicken, turkey
- game birds include: quail, pheasant, partridge, duck
- factors to calculate cooking time include: size, stuffing, cooking method, equipment used
- methods to check for doneness include: temperature readings, sensory assessment
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### I-25.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of poultry and game birds and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with cooking poultry and game birds
- identify hazards, and describe safe work practices and precautions pertaining to cooking poultry and game birds
- identify dietary requirements specific to poultry and game birds
- identify factors to consider when cooking poultry and game birds
- identify cooking methods used to cook poultry, game birds and related dishes, and describe their associated procedures

### I-25.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to cook poultry and game birds

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to cook poultry and game birds
- describe procedures used to cook stuffed poultry and game birds

### I-25.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold, serve and store cooked poultry, game birds and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold, serve and store cooked poultry, game birds and related dishes

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- poultry includes: chicken, turkey
- game birds include: quail, pheasant, partridge, duck
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- factors to consider when cooking include: cut of poultry, muscle structure, degree of doneness, flavouring, salting and seasoning, equipment, temperature, time, yield, resting, use of excess product
- cooking methods include: braising, deep frying, broiling, roasting, combination, sous-vide
