# C-11.04 | Cooks vegetables

**Major Work Activity:** C — Produce
**Task:** C-11 | Prepares vegetables

## Skills

### C-11.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select cooking method

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking method is selected according to type of vegetable, classification, desired result and recipe specifications

### C-11.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to desired results and recipe specifications

### C-11.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** apply techniques to maintain pigmentation techniques to maintain pigmentation and and texture of vegetables according to desired result and recipe specifications

**Evidence of Attainment:** texture of vegetables are applied

### C-11.04.04P
**Performance Criteria:** preserve nutritional value

**Evidence of Attainment:** nutritional value of vegetable is preserved

### C-11.04.05P
**Performance Criteria:** determine doneness

**Evidence of Attainment:** doneness of cooked vegetable is determined according to sensory assessment, desired results and recipe specifications

### C-11.04.06P
**Performance Criteria:** apply cooking techniques to market forms of vegetables

**Evidence of Attainment:** techniques are applied to market forms of vegetables according to manufacturers’ specifications

## Range of Variables

- cooking methods include: blanching, sautéing, grilling, frying, baking, steaming, broiling, roasting , braising type of vegetable is based on colour group (green, white, red, yellow and orange)
- classification includes: leaf , root, tuber, crucif erous
- tools and equipment include: steamer, frying pan, deep fryer, oven, grill, colander
- market forms include: peeled, dehydrated, whole, cut, bulk frozen, IQF, canned

## Knowledge

### C-11.04.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of cooking vegetables

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe cooking methods used to cook vegetables
- identify cooking requirements when cooking vegetables
- identify degree of doneness for vegetables

### C-11.04.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to cook vegetables

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe techniques or ingredients used to maintain pigmentation and textures of vegetables
- describe techniques used to maintain nutritional integrity of vegetable varieties
- describe techniques used to preserve nutritional value of vegetables while cooking

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- cooking methods include: blanching, sautéing, grilling, frying, baking, steaming , broiling, roasting, braising
- cooking requirements include: temperature, timing, batch size
