# D-14.03 | Cooks soups

**Major Work Activity:** D — Stocks, Broths and Soups
**Task:** D-14 | Prepares soups

## Skills

### D-14.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected according to recipe specifications

### D-14.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** combine ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients are combined in sequence according to recipe specifications

### D-14.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** clarify consommés

**Evidence of Attainment:** consommés are clarified according to recipe specifications

### D-14.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** monitor cooking time and aromatics

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking time and aromatics are monitored according to recipe specifications and desired results

### D-14.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** incorporate thickening agents

**Evidence of Attainment:** thickening agents are incorporated to achieve desired results

### D-14.03.06P
**Performance Criteria:** adjust soup characteristics

**Evidence of Attainment:** soup characteristics are adjusted to achieve desired results

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: whisks, food processor, cheese cloth, spoon, spatula, strainers, steam kettle, soup pot
- thickening agents include: roux, purees, modified starches, eggs
- soup characteristics include: taste, thickness, texture, appearance, colour, temperature

## Knowledge

### D-14.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of soups and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with soups
- identify types of soups, and describe their characteristics and serving temperatures
- identify factors to consider when cooking soups
- identify dietary requirements specific to soups
- identify thickening agents

### D-14.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to prepare soups

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to prepare soups
- describe procedures used to clarify consommés
- describe procedures used to thicken soups

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- types of soups include: clear, consommés, cream, puréed, specialty/international, cold
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- thickening agents include: roux, purees, modified starches, eggs
- procedures (to prepare) include: selecting cooking time and temperature, straining, reducing, deglazing, skimming, puréeing
