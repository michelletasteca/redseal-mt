# H-22.01 | Selects seeds and nuts

**Major Work Activity:** H — Grains, Pulses, Seeds, Nuts and Alternative
**Task:** H-22 | Prepares seeds and nuts

## Skills

### H-22.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify and choose types of seeds and nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** types of seeds and nuts are identified and chosen according to recipe specifications and desired results

### H-22.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** determine amount of seeds and nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** amount of seeds and nuts are determined according to recipe specifications

### H-22.01.03P
**Performance Criteria:** determine quality of seeds and nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality of seeds and nuts are determined according to sensory assessment

## Range of Variables

- seeds include: pumpkin, sunflower, flax, chia, quinoa, sesame
- nuts include: tree nuts (almonds, pine, walnuts), groundnuts (peanuts)

## Knowledge

### H-22.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of seeds and nuts and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with seeds and nuts
- identify types of seeds and nuts, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify diff erences between shelled and unshelled seeds and nuts, and describe their characteristics, yield and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to seeds and nuts

### H-22.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to select and handle seeds and nuts

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to select and handle seeds and nuts

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- seeds include: pumpkin, sunflower, flax, chia, quinoa, sesame
- nuts include: tree nuts (almonds, pine, walnuts), groundnuts (peanuts)
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
