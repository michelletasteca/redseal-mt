# H-21.01 | Selects grains and pulses

**Major Work Activity:** H — Grains, Pulses, Seeds, Nuts and Alternative
**Task:** H-21 | Prepares grains and pulses

## Skills

### H-21.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify and choose types of grains and pulses

**Evidence of Attainment:** types of grains and pulses are identified and chosen according to recipe specifications and desired results

### H-21.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** determine quantity of grains and pulses

**Evidence of Attainment:** quantity of grains and pulses is determined according to required yield

### H-21.01.03P
**Performance Criteria:** determine quality of grains and pulses

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality of grains and pulses is determined by performing sensory assessment

## Range of Variables

- grains include: rice (short, long), wild rice, barley, bulgur, oats
- pulses include: lentils, beans, peas

## Knowledge

### H-21.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of grains and pulses and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with grains and pulses
- identify types of grains and pulses, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify differences between processed and dried grains and pulses, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to grains and pulses

### H-21.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to select grains and pulses

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to select grains and pulses
- describe methods to determine yields of grains and pulses
- describe procedures used to handle grains and pulses

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- grains include: rice (short, long), wild rice, barley, bulgur, oats
- pulses include: lentils, beans, peas
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
