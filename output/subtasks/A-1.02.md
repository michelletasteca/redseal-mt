# A-1.02 | Uses personal protective equipment (PPE) and safety equipment

**Major Work Activity:** A — Hygiene, sanitation and safety
**Task:** A-1 | Performs safety and hygiene-related functions

## Skills

### A-1.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** dress in industry-specific clothing and approved footwear

**Evidence of Attainment:** industry-specific clothing and approved footwear are worn to prevent injuries and product contamination

### A-1.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** locate PPE and safety equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** PPE and safety equipment are located according to jurisdictional regulations

### A-1.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** select PPE and safety equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** PPE and safety equipment are selected according to jurisdictional regulations, task to be performed and company policy

### A-1.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** identify worn, defective and expired PPE and safety equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** worn, defective and expired PPE and safety equipment are identified according to jurisdictional regulations and sensory assessment

## Range of Variables

- PPE includes: goggles, respirators, protective gloves (rubber/latex, cut-resistant, heat-resistant), rubber aprons, non-slip protective footwear
- safety equipment includes: fire extinguishers, fire blankets, eye-wash stations, WHMIS stations, first aid kits, Automatic External Defibrillator (AED)
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### A-1.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of PPE and safety equipment, their applications, maintenance and procedures for use

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify types of PPE and safety equipment and describe their applications and procedures for use
- describe procedures used to maintain PPE and safety equipment

### A-1.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of regulatory requirements pertaining to PPE and safety equipment

**Learning Objectives:**
- interpret jurisdictional regulations pertaining to PPE and safety equipment
