# B-3.03 | Uses equipment and appliances

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-3 | Uses tools and equipment

## Skills

### B-3.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select equipment and appliances for use

**Evidence of Attainment:** equipment and appliances are selected for use according to recipe specifications and desired results

### B-3.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** operate equipment and appliances

**Evidence of Attainment:** equipment and appliances are operated according to manufacturers’ specifications

### B-3.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** inspect equipment and appliances

**Evidence of Attainment:** equipment and appliances are inspected for safety and performance

### B-3.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** assemble and disassemble equipment and appliances

**Evidence of Attainment:** equipment and appliances are assembled and disassembled according to manufacturers’ specifications

### B-3.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** store equipment, appliances and accessories

**Evidence of Attainment:** equipment, appliances and accessories are stored according to manufacturers’ specifications, company policies and to ensure safety

### B-3.03.06P
**Performance Criteria:** clean, sanitize and maintain equipment equipment and appliances are cleaned, and appliances manufacturers’ specifications, company policies and jurisdictional regulations

**Evidence of Attainment:** sanitized and maintained according to

## Range of Variables

- equipment and appliances include: stoves, ovens, deep fryers, meat grinders, slicers, mixers, vacuum packaging machines, thermometers, scales, food processors , refrigeration equipment
- maintain equipment and appliances includes: oiling, calibrating, lubricating, cleaning
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### B-3.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of equipment and appliances, their accessories and applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify types of equipment and appliances, and describe their accessories and applications

### B-3.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures to use and maintain equipment and appliances

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures for using equipment and appliances
- describe procedures used to inspect and store equipment and appliances
- identify hazards, and describe safe work practices and precautions pertaining to use of equipment and appliances
- describe procedures used to clean, sanitize and maintain equipment and appliances
