# C-12.02 | Cleans fruit

**Major Work Activity:** C — Produce
**Task:** C-12 | Prepares fruit

## Skills

### C-12.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use cleaning tools

**Evidence of Attainment:** cleaning tools are selected and used according to fruit type and AP conditions

### C-12.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** apply cleaning procedures

**Evidence of Attainment:** cleaning procedures are applied according to fruit type and jurisdictional regulations

### C-12.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain quality of fruit

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality of fruit is maintained during cleaning

### C-12.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** store cleaned fruit

**Evidence of Attainment:** cleaned fruit is stored according to type and procedure to preserve quality

## Range of Variables

- cleaning tools include: gloves, knives, brushes
- cleaning procedures include: washing, paring, brushing, rinsing
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### C-12.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of cleaning fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify types of cleaning tools used to clean fruits
- identify cleaning procedures used to clean fruits

### C-12.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to clean fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe cleaning procedures used to clean fruits
- describe procedures used to maintain quality of fruit during cleaning

### C-12.02.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of storing cleaned fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used for secondary storage to preserve quality of fruit

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- cleaning tools include: gloves, knives, brushes
- cleaning procedures include: washing, paring, brushing, rinsing
