# M-41.03 | Finishes chocolate

**Major Work Activity:** M — Sweet and Savoury Baked Goods and
**Task:** M-41 | Prepares chocolate

## Skills

### M-41.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### M-41.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** apply finishing techniques

**Evidence of Attainment:** finishing techniques are applied to chocolate according to recipe specifications and desired results

### M-41.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** hold and store finished chocolate product

**Evidence of Attainment:** finished chocolate product is held and stored to maintain quality and temper according to desired results

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: tempering machines, bowls, marble slabs, moulds, wooden spoons transfer sheets, acetates, spatulas, cornets, knives, cutters
- finishing techniques include: shaving, coating, brushing, moulding, piping, transfer sheets

## Knowledge

### M-41.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of chocolate and its applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with chocolate
- identify types of chocolate, and describe their properties and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to chocolate
- identify factors to consider when selecting ingredients for chocolates
- identify ingredients used in chocolates, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify market forms (prepared and packaged) of chocolate, and describe their characteristics and applications

### M-41.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of finishing techniques used to finish chocolate

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe finishing techniques used to present and finish chocolate

### M-41.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to hold and store chocolate

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to hold and store chocolate

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- types of chocolate include: white, dark, milk, couverture, coating chocolate, compound, ruby
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- ingredients include: cocoa butter, cocoa powder, inclusions (nuts, fruit), cream, butter, flavourings
- finishing techniques include: shaving, coating, brushing, moulding, piping, transfer sheets
