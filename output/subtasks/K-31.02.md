# K-31.02 | Processes ingredients for condiments, preserves and dressings

**Major Work Activity:** K — Salads and Sandwiches
**Task:** K-31 | Prepares condiments, preserves and dressings

## Skills

### K-31.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use processing tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-31.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** apply processing and finishing techniques

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing and finishing techniques are applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-31.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** apply flavouring, seasonings and thickening agents

**Evidence of Attainment:** flavouring, seasonings and thickening agents are applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-31.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** use safe handling procedures

**Evidence of Attainment:** safe handling procedures are used according to jurisdictional regulations

### K-31.02.05P
**Performance Criteria:** hold and store condiments, preserves and dressings

**Evidence of Attainment:** condiments, preserves and dressings are held and stored according to recipe specifications, desired results and jurisdictional regulations

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: mixers, pots, food processors, blenders, stove, whisk, double-boilers, thermometers, pressure cookers, knives
- processing and finishing techniques include: chopping, dicing, cutting, mixing, blending, cooking, pickling, fermenting, smoking, emulsif ying, whisking, portioning, canning
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations
- condiments include: dips, relishes, syrups, salsas
- preserves include: pickled vegetables, jams, jellies
- dressings include: dairy-based, emulsions, vinaigrette, warm dressings

## Knowledge

### K-31.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of condiments, preserves and dressings, and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with condiments, preserves and dressings
- identify types of condiments, preserves and dressings, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify factors to consider when processing ingredients for condiments, preserves and dressings
- identify dietary requirements specific to condiments, preserves and dressings

### K-31.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of processing and finishing techniques used with ingredients for condiments, preserves and dressings

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe processing and finishing techniques used with ingredients for condiments, preserves and dressings

### K-31.02.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold, store and package condiments, preserves and dressings

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold, store and package condiments, preserves and dressings

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- condiments include: dips, relishes, syrups, salsas
- preserves include: pickled vegetables, jams, jellies
- dressings include: dairy-based, emulsions, vinaigrette, warm dressings
- factors include: mechanical application, type of ingredients, holding time and temperature
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- processing and finishing techniques include: chopping, dicing, cutting, mixing, blending, cooking, pickling, fermenting, smoking, emulsif ying, whisking, portioning, canning
