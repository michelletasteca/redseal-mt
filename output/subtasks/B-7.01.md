# B-7.01 | Uses recipes

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-7 | Performs trade activities

## Skills

### B-7.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** follow recipe sequence

**Evidence of Attainment:** recipe sequence is followed according to recipe specifications

### B-7.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** measure amounts of ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** amounts of ingredients are measured according to recipe specifications

### B-7.01.03P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain consistency when converting recipes

**Evidence of Attainment:** consistency is maintained when converting recipes

### B-7.01.04P
**Performance Criteria:** determine ingredient substitutions

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredient substitutions are determined according to factors

### B-7.01.05P
**Performance Criteria:** adjust seasoning

**Evidence of Attainment:** seasoning is adjusted according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- factors include: menu requirements, dietary requirements, demographics, budget, availability

## Knowledge

### B-7.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of systems of measurement and their conversions

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with systems of measurement
- identify systems of measurement and their abbreviations
- identify tools and equipment used to weigh and measure, and describe their procedures for use
- describe method used to convert between metric, Imperial and American standard
- describe method used to convert between volume and weights

### B-7.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to follow recipes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to follow recipes
- describe procedures used to adjust recipe yields and perform conversions
