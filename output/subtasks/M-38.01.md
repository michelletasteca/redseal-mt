# M-38.01 | Selects ingredients for creams, mousses, frozen desserts, fillings, icings,

**Major Work Activity:** M — Sweet and Savoury Baked Goods and
**Task:** M-38 | Prepares creams, mousses, frozen desserts, fillings,

## Skills

### M-38.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify required ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** required ingredients used in various creams, mousses, frozen desserts, fillings, icings, toppings and sugar work products are identified and chosen according to recipe specifications and desired results

### M-38.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify quality and freshness of ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality and freshness of ingredients are identified according to best before date and sensory assessment
