# M-37.02 | Processes ingredients for batters

**Major Work Activity:** M — Sweet and Savoury Baked Goods and
**Task:** M-37 | Prepares batter-based products

## Skills

### M-37.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### M-37.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** measure ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients are measured according to recipe specifications

### M-37.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** mix ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients are mixed according to recipe specifications and desired results

### M-37.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** apply methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** methods are applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: spatulas, whisks, sif ters, mixers, scales, bowls
- measure includes: weight, volume, size
- ingredients include: flours, fats, egg, sweeteners, liquids, leavening agents, dairy products, specialty products, flavorings, seasonings
- methods include: resting, incorporating fats, creaming, foaming, blending, whipping, two-stage

## Knowledge

### M-37.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of batters used for various batter-based products and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify types of batters, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify types of batter-based products, and describe their characteristics and applications
- define terminology associated with batter
- identify factors to consider when processing ingredients for batters
- identify ingredients used in batters, and describe their functions, characteristics and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to ingredients for batters
- identify market forms (prepared and packaged) of batters, and describe their characteristics and applications

### M-37.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to process ingredients for batters used for various batter-based products

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe methods used to process ingredients for batters used for various batter-based products
- identify factors to consider when determining mixing methods used for batters
- identify factors to consider when determining methods for batter

### M-37.02.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold and store batters

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold and store batter

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- batter-based products include: muffins, cakes, pop-overs, tempura, waffles, pancakes, fritters, corn bread
- ingredients include: flours, fats, egg, sweeteners, liquids, leavening agents, dairy products, specialty products, flavorings, seasonings
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- market forms include: powdered, frozen, fresh
