# L-32.04 | Cooks hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods

**Major Work Activity:** L — Specialty Preparations
**Task:** L-32 | Prepares hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods

## Skills

### L-32.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** determine cooking methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking methods are determined according to hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods, recipe specifications and desired results

### L-32.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** apply cooking methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking methods are applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

### L-32.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** calculate cooking time

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking time is calculated according to type, size and thickness of item, and cooking method according to recipe specifications and desired results

### L-32.04.04P
**Performance Criteria:** determine doneness

**Evidence of Attainment:** doneness is determined by using various methods

## Range of Variables

- cooking methods include: baking, frying, broiling, steaming
- hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods include: canapés, sushi, satays, dim sum
- methods to check for doneness include: temperature readings, sensory assessments

## Knowledge

### L-32.04.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods
- identify types of hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods and describe their characteristics
- identify factors to consider when cooking hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods
- identify dietary requirements specific to ingredients for hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods

### L-32.04.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to cook hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe cooking methods used to cook hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods include: canapés, sushi, satays, dim sum
- factors include: kitchen space, available resources, time management, timing, temperature
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- cooking methods include: baking, frying, broiling, steaming
