# B-6.04 | Maintains inventory

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-6 | Manages products

## Skills

### B-6.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** verify quantities of products

**Evidence of Attainment:** quantities of products are verified to ensure par levels according to business volume

### B-6.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** verify quality of perishable products

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality of perishable products are verified according to sensory assessment and date received

### B-6.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** perform stock rotation

**Evidence of Attainment:** stock rotation is performed according to company policies

### B-6.04.04P
**Performance Criteria:** dispose of expired products

**Evidence of Attainment:** expired products are disposed of according to company policies, jurisdictional regulations and manufacturers’ specifications

## Range of Variables

- products include: dairy, produce, meats, poultry, seafood, dry goods, sundries
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### B-6.04.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of maintaining inventory

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with inspection and storage of products
- explain product rotation method FIFO

### B-6.04.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used for inventory control

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to conduct inventory of products
- explain systems used for inventory control
- describe procedures for verifying product specifications
- describe waste management procedures
- describe concepts of unit and weight

### B-6.04.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of jurisdictional regulations pertaining to safe food handling practices

**Learning Objectives:**
- interpret jurisdictional regulations pertaining to safe food handling practices

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- products include: dairy, produce, meats, poultry, seafood, dry goods, sundries
- product specifications include: market forms, shelf life, case size, unit as purchased, product type, colour
- waste management procedures include: recycling, composting, discard ing, reduce, re-use
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations
