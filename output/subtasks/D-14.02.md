# D-14.02 | Processes soup ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** D — Stocks, Broths and Soups
**Task:** D-14 | Prepares soups

## Skills

### D-14.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications

### D-14.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** calculate quantity of ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** quantity of ingredients for yield are calculated according to recipe specifications

### D-14.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** gather ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** individual ingredients are gathered and accessible in a sequential manner according to recipe specifications

### D-14.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** measure ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients are measured according to recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: scales, cutting boards, knives, whisks, food processors, cheese cloths

## Knowledge

### D-14.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of soups and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with soups
- identify types of soups, and describe their characteristics and serving temperatures
- identify factors to consider when processing ingredients for soups
- identify dietary requirements specific to soups

### D-14.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to process ingredients for soups

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to process ingredients for soups

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- types of soups include: clear, consommés, cream, puréed, specialty/international, cold
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
