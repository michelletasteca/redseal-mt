# C-10.01 | Selects herbs and spices

**Major Work Activity:** C — Produce
**Task:** C-10 | Prepares herbs and spices

## Skills

### C-10.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify indicators of freshness and quality

**Evidence of Attainment:** indicators of freshness and quality are identified according to desired results

### C-10.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify herbs and spices

**Evidence of Attainment:** herbs and spices to complement flavours in recipe are identified

### C-10.01.03P
**Performance Criteria:** choose herbs and spices

**Evidence of Attainment:** herbs and spices are chosen according to desired results

## Range of Variables

- indicators of freshness and quality include: hydration, colour, texture
- herbs include: rosemary, mint, thyme, basil
- spices include: peppercorn, nutmeg, cumin

## Knowledge

### C-10.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of herbs and spices, their characteristics, applications and procedures for use

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify herbs and spices used to enhance flavour of foods, and describe their characteristics, applications and procedures for use
- identify types of herbs and spices, and describe their characteristics, applications and procedures for use
- describe indicators of freshness and quality when selecting herbs and spices

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- herbs include: rosemary, mint, thyme, basil
- spices include: peppercorn, nutmeg, cumin
- types of herbs and spices include: dry, fresh, frozen, paste
- indicators of freshness and quality include: hydration, colour, texture
