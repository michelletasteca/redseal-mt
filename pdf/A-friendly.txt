MAJOR WORK ACTIVITY A Hygiene, sanitation and safety TASK A-1 Performs safety and hygiene-related functions TASK DESCRIPTOR Cooks are expected to perform safety-related functions throughout all activities of the trade. They maintain, clean and sanitize tools and equipment to ensure a safe and hygienic workplace. They use personal protective equipment (PPE) and safety equipment by complying with codes and regulations such as the Workplace Hazardous Materials Information System (WHMIS). Cooks must constantly maintain personal hygiene.
