# H-22.02 | Processes seeds and nuts

**Major Work Activity:** H — Grains, Pulses, Seeds, Nuts and Alternative
**Task:** H-22 | Prepares seeds and nuts

## Skills

### H-22.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** inspect and clean seeds and nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** seeds and nuts are inspected and cleaned to ensure they are free of contaminants

### H-22.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** discard seeds and nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** seeds and nuts that show appearance of chemical and biological contamination are discarded

### H-22.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** process seeds and nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** seeds and nuts are processed using methods according to recipe specifications

### H-22.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** blanch nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** nuts are blanched according to recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- seeds include: pumpkin, sunflower, flax, chia, quinoa, sesame
- nuts include: tree nuts (almonds, pine, walnuts), groundnuts (peanuts)
- contaminants include: shell, twigs, insects
- methods to process include: soaking, straining, grinding, roasting, blanching, chopping, shelling

## Knowledge

### H-22.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of seeds and nuts and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with seeds and nuts
- identify types of seeds and nuts, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to seeds and nuts

### H-22.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to process seeds and nuts

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to clean seeds and nuts
- describe procedures used and length of time required to soak seeds and nuts
- describe methods to process seeds and nuts

### H-22.02.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle seeds and nuts

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle seeds and nuts

- seeds include: pumpkin, sunflower, flax, chia, quinoa, sesame
