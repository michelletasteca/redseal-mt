# C-12.03 | Processes fruit

**Major Work Activity:** C — Produce
**Task:** C-12 | Prepares fruit

## Skills

### C-12.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to desired results and recipe specifications

### C-12.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify processing methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing methods are identified according to recipe specifications and desired results

### C-12.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain uniform cuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** uniform cuts to fruit are maintained for consistency in appearance and cooking

### C-12.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** cut shapes of fruit for garnishes

**Evidence of Attainment:** fruit is cut in shapes for garnishes according to desired result and recipe specifications

### C-12.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain quality of fruit

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality of fruit is maintained during processing

### C-12.03.06P
**Performance Criteria:** minimize waste and utilize trim

**Evidence of Attainment:** waste is minimized and trim is utilized to maximize yield

### C-12.03.07P
**Performance Criteria:** store cut fruit

**Evidence of Attainment:** cut fruit is stored according to type and procedure to preserve quality of stored fruit

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: knives, mandolins, shape cutters, juicer, melon baller, spoon, food processor, fruit carving tools, blender
- methods include: cutting, acidulating, juicing, puréeing, shaping, shaving, grating, slicing
- quality of stored fruit includes: minimal oxidation, colour, flavour, texture

## Knowledge

### C-12.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of processing fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify tools and equipment used to process fruits
- identify types of cuts and describe their characteristics, applications and associated procedures
- identify methods used to modify textures of fruits

### C-12.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to process fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe techniques used to carve fruits
- describe procedures used to maintain quality of fruit during processing
- describe processing methods used for fruits and to minimize waste
- describe uses and applications for trim

### C-12.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of storing processed fruits

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used for secondary storage to preserve quality of fruit

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- tools and equipment include: knives, mandolins, shape cutters, juicer, melon baller, spoon, food processor, fruit carving tools, blender
- types of cuts include: julienne, baton, brunoise, dice, supreme
- methods include: cutting, acidulating, juicing, puréeing, shaping, shaving, grating, slicing
