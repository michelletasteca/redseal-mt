# I-24.04 | Finishes meats and game meats

**Major Work Activity:** I — Meats, Game Meats, Poultry, Game Birds
**Task:** I-24 | Prepares meats and game meats

## Skills

### I-24.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** rest cooked meats and game meats

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooked meats and game meats are rested according to size, weight and internal temperature to retain yield and moisture, and allow for carryover cooking

### I-24.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** apply finishing techniques

**Evidence of Attainment:** finishing techniques are applied according to recipe specifications

### I-24.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** determine and apply carving and portioning techniques

**Evidence of Attainment:** carving and portioning techniques are determined and applied according to type, grain of muscle, size of cut to maximize yield and tenderness, to control portions, and for presentation value

### I-24.04.04P
**Performance Criteria:** present with accompaniments

**Evidence of Attainment:** meats and game meats are presented with accompaniments according to recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- meats include: pork, veal, beef , lamb, goat
- game meats include: bison, elk, caribou, moose, deer, kangaroo, musk ox, wild boar, rabbit
- finishing techniques include: broiling, crusting, basting, glazing, saucing, seasoning
- accompaniments include: sauces, glazes, condiments, garnishes, compound butters, vegetables, herbs, starches
