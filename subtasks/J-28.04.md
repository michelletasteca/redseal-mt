# J-28.04 | Finishes seafood

**Major Work Activity:** J — Fish and Seafood
**Task:** J-28 | Prepares seafood

## Skills

### J-28.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** apply finishing techniques

**Evidence of Attainment:** finishing techniques are applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

### J-28.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** adjust seasoning

**Evidence of Attainment:** seasoning is adjusted according to recipe specifications and desired results

### J-28.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** garnish

**Evidence of Attainment:** seafood is garnished according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- finishing techniques include: broiling, crusting, resting, glazing, torching, smoking, saucing, presentation seafood (various formats) includes (for this task does not include fin fish): clams, oysters, mussels, lobster, crayfish, shrimp, squid, octopus, jellyfish, roe,

## Knowledge

### J-28.04.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of seafood and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with seafood
- identify hazards, and describe safe work practices and precautions pertaining to serving, handling, and storing seafood
- identify dietary requirements specific to seafood

### J-28.04.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to finish seafood and seafood dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe finishing techniques, including garnishes and accompaniments

### J-28.04.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold, serve and store seafood

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold, serve and store seafood

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- seafood (various formats) includes (for this task does not include fin fish): clams, oysters, mussels, lobster, crayfish, shrimp, squid, octopus, jellyfish, roe,
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- finishing techniques include: broiling, crusting, resting, glazing, torching, smoking, saucing, presentation
