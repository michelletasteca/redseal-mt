# E-15.02 | Processes thickening and binding ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** E — Sauces
**Task:** E-15 | Prepares thickening and binding agents

## Skills

### E-15.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications

### E-15.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** calculate ratio of thickening and binding ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ratio of thickening and binding ingredients is calculated according to desired results

### E-15.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** incorporate thickening and binding ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** thickening and binding ingredients are incorporated according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: bowls, whisks, ladles, pots, strainer, blender

## Knowledge

### E-15.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of thickening and binding agents and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with thickening and binding agents
- identify types of thickening and binding agents, and describe their applications and procedures for use
- identify factors to consider when processing ingredients for thickening and binding agents
- identify ingredients and ratios used to process thickening and binding agents
- identify dietary requirements specific to ingredients for thickening and binding agents

### E-15.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to prepare thickening and binding agents

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to prepare thickening and binding agents

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- thickening and binding agents include: roux, starches, liaison, beurre manié, vegetable purées, reduction
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
