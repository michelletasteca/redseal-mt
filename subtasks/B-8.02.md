# B-8.02 | Selects ingredients to meet dietary requirements

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-8 | Adapts cooking practices to meet dietary requirements

## Skills

### B-8.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify dietary requirements

**Evidence of Attainment:** dietary requirements are identified and documented

### B-8.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** propose alternate dish

**Evidence of Attainment:** alternate dish is proposed to meet dietary requirements if recipe cannot be modified

### B-8.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** select substitute ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** substitute ingredients are selected according to dietary requirements

## Range of Variables

- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences

## Knowledge

### B-8.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of selecting ingredients to meet dietary requirements substitutes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe food ingredients, allergens and identify factors to consider when selecting alternative ingredients

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- factors include: allergies and intolerances, variety, colour, dietary requirements, nutrition, use of product, cooking methods, clientele/demographics
