# L-34.03 | Finishes gels and glazes

**Major Work Activity:** L — Specialty Preparations
**Task:** L-34 | Prepares gels and glazes

## Skills

### L-34.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use finishing tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** finishing tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### L-34.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** use finishing techniques

**Evidence of Attainment:** finishing techniques are used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### L-34.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** hold and store gels and glazes

**Evidence of Attainment:** gels and glazes are held and stored according to recipe specifications, desired results and jurisdictional regulations

## Range of Variables

- finishing tools and equipment include: brushes, bain-marie, bowls
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### L-34.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of finishing gels and glazes and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with gels and glazes
- identify factors to consider when finishing gels and glazes
- identify dietary requirements specific to gels and glazes

### L-34.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to finish gels and glazes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe methods used to finish gels and glazes

### L-34.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold and store gels and glazes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold and store gels and glazes

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- factors include: temperature, shine, viscosity
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- methods include: combining, cutting, portioning, presenting, unmoulding
