# K-30.01 | Selects salad ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** K — Salads and Sandwiches
**Task:** K-30 | Prepares salads

## Skills

### K-30.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** identify indicators of freshness and quality

**Evidence of Attainment:** indicators of freshness and quality are identified according to sensory assessment

### K-30.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify salad ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** salad ingredients are identified according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- indicators of freshness and quality include: hydration, colour, texture, best before dates
- ingredients include: vegetables, fruit, proteins, accompaniments, starches, grains, dressings, cheese, pulses, seeds

## Knowledge

### K-30.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of salads and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with salads
- identify types of warm and cold salads, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify factors to consider when selecting ingredients for salads
- identify dietary requirements specific to salad ingredients

### K-30.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to select salad ingredients

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to select salad ingredients

### K-30.01.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold and store ingredients for salads

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold and store ingredients for salads

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- factors include: integrity, seasonality, texture, local availability, viscosity, shelf life, size
- ingredients include: vegetables, fruit, proteins, accompaniments, starches, grains, dressings, cheese, pulses, seeds
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
