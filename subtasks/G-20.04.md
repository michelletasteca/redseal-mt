# G-20.04 | Finishes stuffed pastas and dumplings

**Major Work Activity:** G — Pastas, Noodles, Stuffed Pastas and
**Task:** G-20 | Prepares stuffed pastas and dumplings

## Skills

### G-20.04.01P
**Performance Criteria:** prepare stuffed pastas and dumplings for serving

**Evidence of Attainment:** stuffed pastas and dumplings are prepared for serving using reheating methods

### G-20.04.02P
**Performance Criteria:** select sauces, dressings, components and accompaniments

**Evidence of Attainment:** sauces, dressings, components and accompaniments are selected according to recipe specifications

### G-20.04.03P
**Performance Criteria:** adjust seasonings

**Evidence of Attainment:** seasoning types and amounts are adjusted according to recipe specifications and sensory assessment

### G-20.04.04P
**Performance Criteria:** garnish stuffed pastas and dumplings

**Evidence of Attainment:** stuffed pastas and dumplings are garnished according to recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- stuffed pastas include: cannelloni, manicotti, tortellini, ravioli, agnolloti, tortelloni
- dumplings include: wontons, pierogi, matzo balls, gyoza, shumai, gnocchi, market forms (prepared and packaged)
- reheating methods include: boiling, simmering, poaching, steaming, frying, sautéing
