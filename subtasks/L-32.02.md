# L-32.02 | Processes ingredients for hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods

**Major Work Activity:** L — Specialty Preparations
**Task:** L-32 | Prepares hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods

## Skills

### L-32.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use processing tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### L-32.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** apply processing methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing methods are applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

### L-32.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** cook hors d’oeuvres and other finger food ingredients and components

**Evidence of Attainment:** hors d’oeuvres and other finger food ingredients and components are cooked according to recipe specifications and desired results

### L-32.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** store hors d’oeuvres and other finger food ingredients and components

**Evidence of Attainment:** hors d’oeuvres and other finger food ingredients and components are stored for future assembly according to jurisdictional regulations and desired results

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: food processors, cutting boards, knives, pans, bowls, stove
- methods include: wrapping, mixing, folding, baking, cutting, poaching, frying, blending
- hors d’oeuvres ingredients and components include: vol-au-vent, tart shells, breads, rice, garnish, meats, vegetables, fruit, cheeses, crackers, edible wrapping
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations

## Knowledge

### L-32.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods
- identify factors to consider when processing ingredients for hors d’oeuvres and other finger foods
- identify dietary requirements specific to ingredients for hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods

### L-32.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to prepare hors d’oeuvres and other finger food ingredients

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe methods used to process hors d’oeuvres and other finger food ingredients

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- hors d'oeuvres and other finger foods include: canapés, sushi, satays, dim sum
- factors include: texture, flavour, type of service, available resources
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- methods include: wrapping, mixing, folding, baking, cutting, poaching, frying, blending
