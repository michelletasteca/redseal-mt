# C-11.03 | Processes vegetables

**Major Work Activity:** C — Produce
**Task:** C-11 | Prepares vegetables

## Skills

### C-11.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to desired results and recipe specifications

### C-11.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify processing methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** processing methods are identified according to recipe specifications and desired results

### C-11.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain uniform cuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** uniform cuts to vegetable are maintained for consistency in appearance and cooking

### C-11.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** perform various types of cuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** various types of cuts are performed according to desired result and appearance

### C-11.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** cut and shape vegetable for garnishes

**Evidence of Attainment:** vegetable is cut and shaped for garnishes according to desired result and appearance

### C-11.03.06P
**Performance Criteria:** maintain quality of vegetable

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality of vegetable is maintained during processing

### C-11.03.07P
**Performance Criteria:** minimize waste and utilize trim

**Evidence of Attainment:** waste is minimized and trim is utilized to maximize yield

### C-11.03.08P
**Performance Criteria:** store cut vegetables

**Evidence of Attainment:** cut vegetables are stored according to type and procedure to preserve quality

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: knives, crinkle cutters, mandolins, food processors, slicer, juicer, blender, vegetable carving tools
- methods include: cutting, acidulating, juicing, puréeing, shaping, shaving, grating, slicing , drying
- types of cuts include: julienne, baton, brunoise, dice

## Knowledge

### C-11.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of processing vegetables

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify tools and equipment used to process vegetables
- identify types of cuts, and describe their characteristics, applications and associated procedures
- identify methods used to modify textures of vegetables

### C-11.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to process vegetables

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe techniques used to carve vegetables
- describe methods used to process vegetables and to minimize waste
- describe procedures used to maintain quality of vegetables during processing
- describe uses and applications for trim

### C-11.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of storing processed vegetables

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used for secondary storage to preserve quality of vegetables
