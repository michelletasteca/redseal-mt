# H-22.03 | Cooks seeds and nuts

**Major Work Activity:** H — Grains, Pulses, Seeds, Nuts and Alternative
**Task:** H-22 | Prepares seeds and nuts

## Skills

### H-22.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### H-22.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** select ingredients and seasonings

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients and seasonings are selected according to recipe specifications

### H-22.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** use cooking methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking methods are used according to recipe specifications

### H-22.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** determine doneness

**Evidence of Attainment:** doneness is determined by performing sensory assessment

### H-22.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** finish seeds and nuts

**Evidence of Attainment:** seeds and nuts are finished using methods according to recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: colanders, steamers, pans (hotel, frying, sheet), pots, tongs, spatula, pressure cooker
- cooking methods include: boiling, steaming, roasting, baking, frying, simmering, pilaf
- seeds include: pumpkin, sunflower, flax, chia, quinoa, sesame
- nuts include: tree nuts (almonds, pine, walnuts), groundnuts (peanuts)
- methods for finishing include: puréeing, seasoning, curing, candying

## Knowledge

### H-22.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of seeds and nuts, and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with seeds and nuts
- identify types of seeds and nuts, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to seeds and nuts

### H-22.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to prepare seeds, nuts and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe cooking methods used to prepare seeds, nuts and related dishes

### H-22.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to finish seeds, nuts and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe finishing and presentation techniques specific to seeds and nuts

### H-22.03.04L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle and store cooked seeds, nuts and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle and store cooked seeds, nuts and related dishes

- seeds include: pumpkin, sunflower, flax, chia, quinoa, sesame
