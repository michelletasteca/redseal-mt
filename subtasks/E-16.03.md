# E-16.03 | Cooks sauces

**Major Work Activity:** E — Sauces
**Task:** E-16 | Prepares sauces

## Skills

### E-16.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** combine ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients are combined in sequence according to recipe specifications

### E-16.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** adjust temperature of sauce

**Evidence of Attainment:** temperature of sauce is adjusted according to desired results

### E-16.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** use cooking methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking methods are used according to recipe specifications

### E-16.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** deglaze cooking vessel

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking vessel is deglazed by adding liquid to base to capture flavour profile

### E-16.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** determine sauce qualities

**Evidence of Attainment:** sauce qualities are determined according to desired results

### E-16.03.06P
**Performance Criteria:** apply thickening technique

**Evidence of Attainment:** thickening technique is applied according to desired results

### E-16.03.07P
**Performance Criteria:** adjust sauce qualities

**Evidence of Attainment:** sauce qualities are adjusted according to desired results

## Range of Variables

- cooking methods include: tempering, reduction, blending, infusion, coagulation, caramelization
- sauce qualities include: consistency, colour, flavour, texture
- thickening technique includes: reduction, adding thickening agents (roux, liaison, slurry), emulsif ication

## Knowledge

### E-16.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of sauces and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with sauces
- identify types of mother sauces and their derivatives, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify types of other sauces, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify butter sauces and compound butters, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify factors to consider when cooking sauces
- identify ingredients and ratios used in sauces
- identify dietary requirements specific to sauces

### E-16.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to prepare sauces

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to prepare hot sauces
- describe procedures used to prepare cold sauces

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- mother sauces include: espagnole, béchamel, velouté, tomato, hollandaise
- derivatives include: demi-glace, bearnaise, mornay, supreme, portugaise
- other sauces include: chutneys, salsas, pan gravies, jus lié, reductions, curries, soy -based, coulis, chocolate, caramel
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
