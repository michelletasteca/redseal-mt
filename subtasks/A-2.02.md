# A-2.02 | Cools food rapidly

**Major Work Activity:** A — Hygiene, sanitation and safety
**Task:** A-2 | Practices food safety procedures

## Skills

### A-2.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select cooling methods and vessels

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooling methods and vessels are selected for rapid cooling

### A-2.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** monitor cooling process

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooling process is monitored to ensure that targeted temperature is reached within allotted time according to jurisdictional regulations

### A-2.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** take corrective measures

**Evidence of Attainment:** corrective measures are taken by adjusting the cooling process to ensure required temperature is reached using various methods or taking further actions according to company policies and jurisdictional regulations

## Range of Variables

- methods include: ice bath, divide product into smaller batches, place product in refrigerated unit, use ice wands, use blast chiller
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations
- further actions include: recording, disposing of item, informing supervisor

## Knowledge

### A-2.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of methods used describe cooling process for products to cool foods rapidly describe methods for cooling foods to required temperature in a required time frame while maintaining quality and safety of product identify temperatures required for cold holding of food describe danger zone temperatures


### A-2.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of jurisdictional regulations

**Learning Objectives:**
- interpret jurisdictional regulations
