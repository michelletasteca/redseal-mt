# B-6.01 | Orders products

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-6 | Manages products

## Skills

### B-6.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** determine product specifications

**Evidence of Attainment:** product specifications are determined according to dietary requirements and menu specifications

### B-6.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify ingredient requirements and complete order list

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredient requirements are identified and order list is completed according to production needs, dietary requirements and menu specifications

### B-6.01.03P
**Performance Criteria:** determine quantities required

**Evidence of Attainment:** quantities of products required are determined according to business volume and delivery schedule

### B-6.01.04P
**Performance Criteria:** relay product order and specifications to product supplier

**Evidence of Attainment:** product order and specifications are relayed to product supplier according to production needs

### B-6.01.05P
**Performance Criteria:** interpret vendor product listings

**Evidence of Attainment:** vendor product listings are interpreted to determine if supplier has required inventory

## Range of Variables

- product specifications include: market forms, shelf life, case size, product type, colour
- products include: dairy, produce, meats, poultry, seafood, dry goods, sundries
- vendors include: major suppliers, local community suppliers, foragers

## Knowledge

### B-6.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of vendors and products

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify vendors of products
- identify product based on sustainability, and seasonal and local availability
- describe product specification factors
- identify types of vendors

### B-6.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to order products

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to order products
- describe product specifications
- describe ordering methods

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- vendors include: major suppliers, local community suppliers, foragers
- products include: dairy, produce, meats, poultry, seafood, dry goods, sundries
- product specifications include: market forms, shelf life, case size, product type, colour
- ordering methods include: phone, electronic, face-to-face
