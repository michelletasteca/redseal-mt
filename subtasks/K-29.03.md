# K-29.03 | Assembles sandwiches

**Major Work Activity:** K — Salads and Sandwiches
**Task:** K-29 | Prepares sandwiches

## Skills

### K-29.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use sandwich assembly tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** sandwich assembly tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-29.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** plan assembly steps

**Evidence of Attainment:** assembly steps are planned according to recipe specifications and desired results

### K-29.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** combine sandwich filling, toppings, condiments and bread

**Evidence of Attainment:** sandwich filling, toppings, condiments and bread are combined according to sandwich style, recipe specifications and desired results

### K-29.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** apply finishing techniques to sandwiches

**Evidence of Attainment:** finishing techniques are applied to sandwiches according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- sandwich assembly tools and equipment include: toasters, knives, paper, picks, spreaders
- finishing techniques include: garnishing, packaging, presentation, cutting

## Knowledge

### K-29.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of sandwiches and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with sandwiches
- identify types of sandwiches and describe their characteristics
- identify factors to consider when assembling sandwiches
- identify and describe components of sandwiches
- identify dietary requirements specific to sandwich ingredients

### K-29.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to assemble sandwiches

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to assemble sandwiches

### K-29.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures to finish sandwiches

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe styles of service associated with sandwiches
- describe presentation and finishing techniques

### K-29.03.04L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold and store sandwiches

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold and store sandwiches

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- types of sandwiches include: hamburgers, hot dogs, paninis, shawarma, wraps, picnic, finger, pizza, deli
- factors include: cross-contamination, holding time and method, temperature of ingredients, presentation, portion control
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- finishing techniques include: garnishing, packaging, presentation, cutting
