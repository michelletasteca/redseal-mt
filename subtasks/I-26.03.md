# I-26.03 | Cooks variety meats

**Major Work Activity:** I — Meats, Game Meats, Poultry, Game Birds
**Task:** I-26 | Prepares variety meats

## Skills

### I-26.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** choose and apply cooking method

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking method is chosen and applied according to recipe specifications and desired results

### I-26.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** calculate cooking time

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking time is calculated according to type, size and thickness of variety meats according to recipe specifications and desired results

### I-26.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** determine doneness

**Evidence of Attainment:** doneness is determined by using various methods

## Range of Variables

- cooking methods include: poaching, simmering, grilling, sautéing, braising, frying, sous -vide
- variety meats include: sweetbreads, liver, heart, kidney, tongue, tripe
- methods for determining doneness include: temperature readings, sensory assessment
