# E-15.01 | Selects thickening and binding ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** E — Sauces
**Task:** E-15 | Prepares thickening and binding agents

## Skills

### E-15.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** determine thickening and binding agents to be made

**Evidence of Attainment:** thickening and binding agents to be made are determined according to recipe specifications and desired results

### E-15.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify indicators of freshness and quality of ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** indicators of freshness and quality of ingredients are identified according to sensory assessment

### E-15.01.032P
**Performance Criteria:** identify and choose ingredients for thickening and binding agents

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients for thickening and binding agents are identified and chosen according to recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- thickening and binding agents include: roux, starches, liaison, beurre manié, vegetable purées, reduction
- indicators of freshness and quality include: firmness, colour, texture

## Knowledge

### E-15.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of thickening and binding agents and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with thickening and binding agents
- identify types of thickening and binding agents, and describe their applications and procedures for use
- identify factors to consider when selecting ingredients for thickening and binding agents
- identify dietary requirements specific to ingredients for thickening and binding agents

### E-15.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to select ingredients for thickening and binding agents

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to select ingredients for thickening and binding agents

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- thickening and binding agents include: roux, starches, liaison, beurre manié, vegetable purées, reduction
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
