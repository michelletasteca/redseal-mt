# G-19.01 | Selects pasta and noodle dish ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** G — Pastas, Noodles, Stuffed Pastas and
**Task:** G-19 | Prepares pastas and noodles

## Skills

### G-19.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** determine quality of ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** quality of ingredients is determined by identifying freshness and checking best before dates

### G-19.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify and choose ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients are identified and chosen for fillings and pasta or noodle dishes according to recipe specifications

### G-19.01.03P
**Performance Criteria:** determine dough type and styles required RANGE OF VARIABLE fillings include: cheese, meat, vegetable dough types include: rice based, corn based, buckwheat, wheat flour, egg based styles include: spaghetti, ravioli, vermiccelli, penne, udon, lo mein, couscous

**Evidence of Attainment:** dough type and style required for dishes are determined according to recipe specifications

## Knowledge

### G-19.01.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of pastas and noodles and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with pastas and noodles and their associated dishes
- identify types of pastas and noodles, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to pasta or noodle dishes
- identify dough types, and describe their characteristics and applications

### G-19.01.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to select ingredients for pasta and noodle dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to select ingredients for pasta and noodle dishes
