# H-21.03 | Cooks grains and pulses

**Major Work Activity:** H — Grains, Pulses, Seeds, Nuts and Alternative
**Task:** H-21 | Prepares grains and pulses

## Skills

### H-21.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### H-21.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** select ingredients and seasonings

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients and seasonings are selected according to recipe specifications

### H-21.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** use cooking methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking methods are used according to recipe specifications

### H-21.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** determine doneness

**Evidence of Attainment:** doneness is determined by performing sensory assessment

### H-21.03.05P
**Performance Criteria:** finish grains and pulses

**Evidence of Attainment:** grains and pulses are finished using methods according to recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- tools and equipment include: colanders, steamers, pots, tongs, spatula, Dutch ovens, woks, pressure cooker, spoons
- cooking methods include: boiling, steaming, baking, stir-frying, puffing, pressure cooking, pilaf and risotto methods, braising
- grains include: rice (short, long), wild rice, barley, bulgur, oats
- pulses include: lentils, beans, peas

## Knowledge

### H-21.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of grains and pulses and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with grains and pulses
- identify types of grains and pulses, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify dietary requirements specific to grains and pulses

### H-21.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to prepare grains and pulses, and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to prepare grains and pulses, and related dishes
- describe methods to determine yields of grains and pulses

### H-21.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures to finish grains and pulses, and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe finishing and presentation techniques

### H-21.03.04L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to cool or hold grains and pulses, and related dishes

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to cool or hold grains and pulses, and related dishes

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- grains include: rice (short, long), wild rice, barley, bulgur, oats
- pulses include: lentils, beans, peas
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- dishes include: tabbouleh, red beans and rice, pilaf , porridge, dahl, congee
