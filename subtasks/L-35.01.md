# L-35.01 | Selects marinade, rub and brine ingredients

**Major Work Activity:** L — Specialty Preparations
**Task:** L-35 | Prepares marinades, rubs and brines

## Skills

### L-35.01.01P
**Performance Criteria:** choose marinade, rub or brine

**Evidence of Attainment:** marinade, rub or brine is chosen according to type of protein, vegetables or fruit being used, recipe specifications and desired results

### L-35.01.02P
**Performance Criteria:** identify and choose ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients are identified and chosen according to recipe specifications and desired results

## Range of Variables

- marinades include: vinegar-based, salt-based, fruit-based, alcohol-based
- rubs include: dry seasoning, pastes, mustard-based, sugar-based
