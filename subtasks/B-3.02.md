# B-3.02 | Uses pots, pans and utensils

**Major Work Activity:** B — Common Occupational Skills
**Task:** B-3 | Uses tools and equipment

## Skills

### B-3.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select pots, pans and utensils

**Evidence of Attainment:** pots, pans and utensils are selected according to desired results and recipe specifications

### B-3.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** utilize pots, pans and utensils

**Evidence of Attainment:** pots, pans and utensils are utilized according to desired results, recipe specifications and manufacturers’ specifications

### B-3.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** inspect pots, pans and utensils

**Evidence of Attainment:** pots, pans and utensils are inspected according to company policies and jurisdictional regulations

### B-3.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** wash, sanitize and dry pots, pans and utensils

**Evidence of Attainment:** pots, pans and utensils are washed, sanitized and dried according to jurisdictional regulations

### B-3.02.05P
**Performance Criteria:** store pots, pans and utensils

**Evidence of Attainment:** pots, pans and utensils are stored to maximize efficiency, and to prevent damage and unsanitary conditions

### B-3.02.06P
**Performance Criteria:** choose material appropriate utensils to prevent damage to pots and pans

**Evidence of Attainment:** material appropriate utensils are chosen to prevent damage to pots and pans

### B-3.02.07P
**Performance Criteria:** control heat

**Evidence of Attainment:** heat is controlled to prevent damage to pots, pans and utensils

## Range of Variables

- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations
- material appropriate utensils include: hard wood, high heat resistant silicone, stainless steel, non-stick coated, bamboo

## Knowledge

### B-3.02.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of pots, pans and utensils, and their characteristics and applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- identify types of pots and pans, and describe their characteristics and applications
- identify materials used in pots and pans
- identify types of utensils, their characteristics and applications
- identify material appropriate utensils

### B-3.02.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures to use pots, pans and utensils

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures to use different types of pots and pans
- describe procedures to use different types of utensils
- describe procedures used to inspect and store pots, pans and utensils
- describe correct placement of pots and pans on stove top or in oven
- identify hazards, and describe safe work practices and precautions pertaining to use of pots, pans and utensils
- describe procedures used to wash, sanitize and dry pots, pans and utensils
- describe treatment methods for various types of pots, pans and utensils

### B-3.02.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of regulatory requirements regarding use of pots, pans and utensils

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe jurisdictional regulations regarding use of pots, pans and utensils

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- types of pots and pans include: braising, roasting, sauté, stock, brazier, wok
- materials used in pots and pans include: copper, stainless steel, aluminum, non-stick material, cast iron, ceramic, stoneware
- types of utensils include: wooden spoons, whisks, spatulas, scissors, tongs, tweezers
- material appropriate utensils include: hard wood, high heat resistant silicone, stainless steel, non-stick coated, bamboo
- jurisdictional regulations include: WHMIS, HACCP, OH&S, public health regulations
