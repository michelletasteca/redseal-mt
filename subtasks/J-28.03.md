# J-28.03 | Cooks seafood

**Major Work Activity:** J — Fish and Seafood
**Task:** J-28 | Prepares seafood

## Skills

### J-28.03.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use cooking methods

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking methods are selected and used according to type of seafood, recipe specifications and desired results

### J-28.03.02P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use tools and equipment

**Evidence of Attainment:** tools and equipment are selected and used according to type of seafood, recipe specifications and desired results

### J-28.03.03P
**Performance Criteria:** calculate cooking time

**Evidence of Attainment:** cooking time for seafood is calculated according to size and cooking methods to achieve desired results

### J-28.03.04P
**Performance Criteria:** determine seafood doneness

**Evidence of Attainment:** seafood doneness is determined by using methods to determine doneness

## Range of Variables

- cooking methods include: poaching, steaming, pan searing, deep-frying, pan-frying, broiling, braising, baking, smoking seafood includes (for this task does not include fin fish): clams, oysters, mussels, lobster, crayfish, shrimp, squid, octopus, jellyfish, roe
- tools and equipment include: pans, pots, steamers, smokers, ovens, grills , deep-fryers
- methods to determine doneness include: temperature readings, needle test, sensory assessment

## Knowledge

### J-28.03.01L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of seafood and their applications

**Learning Objectives:**
- define terminology associated with seafood
- identify hazards, and describe safe work practices and precautions pertaining to cooking seafood
- identify factors to consider when cooking seafood
- identify dietary requirements specific to seafood

### J-28.03.02L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to cook seafood

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to determine doneness of seafood
- identify cooking methods used to cook seafood and describe their associated procedures

### J-28.03.03L
**Learning Outcomes:** demonstrate knowledge of procedures used to handle, hold and store cooked seafood

**Learning Objectives:**
- describe procedures used to handle, hold and store cooked seafood

## Range of Variables (Knowledge)

- seafood includes (for this task does not include fin fish): clams, oysters, mussels, lobster, crayfish, shrimp, squid, octopus, jellyfish, roe
- factors include: variety, size, in-shell, out-of -shell
- dietary requirements include: cultural, allergies, ethical, religious, health-related, environmental, preferences
- cooking methods include: poaching, steaming, pan searing, deep-frying, pan-frying, broiling, braising, baking, smoking
