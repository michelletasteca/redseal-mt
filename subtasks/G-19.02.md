**Task:** G-19 | Prepares pastas and noodles

## Skills

### G-19.02.01P
**Performance Criteria:** select and use pasta and noodle- processing machines and tools

**Evidence of Attainment:** pasta and noodle-processing machines and tools are selected and used according to recipe specifications, desired results and manufacturers’ specifications

### G-19.02.02P
**Performance Criteria:** process ingredients

**Evidence of Attainment:** ingredients for assembly and fillings are processed and prepared according to recipe specifications

### G-19.02.03P
**Performance Criteria:** assemble pastas and noodles

**Evidence of Attainment:** pastas and noodles are assembled according to recipe specifications

### G-19.02.04P
**Performance Criteria:** use pasta making procedures

**Evidence of Attainment:** pasta making procedures are used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### G-19.02.05P
**Performance Criteria:** use hand forming techniques

**Evidence of Attainment:** hand forming techniques are used according to recipe specifications and desired results

### G-19.02.06P
**Performance Criteria:** store and hold prepared pastas and noodles

**Evidence of Attainment:** prepared pastas and noodles are stored and held according to service requirements and recipe specifications

## Range of Variables

- pasta and noodle processing machines and tools include: ravioli press, electric pasta roller, cutter, blades, bowls, drying racks, crimper
- ingredients include: cheese, proteins, vegetables
- procedures include: cutting, drying, extruding, blanching, freezing, assembly, soaking
- hand forming techniques include: rolling, folding, stretching, cutting, stuffing, pulling

- describe procedures used to handle and store prepared pastas and noodles
